Life of a Starfish

Starfish, unique to most animals, have five arms that spread from a centralized disk, providing it a beautiful star-like appearance. The capacity of starfish to grow more arms is one wonderful quality. Starfish are great masters of regeneration because they are capable of creating a new arm if one is lost due to harm or attack. Their strong arms and tube feet can be used to break apart shells, particularly those of clams and mussels. The starfish splits up its shell and pushes its stomach into its prey, where it physically digests it.
